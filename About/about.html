Our Mission

Having successfully operating our first center at MAHARASHTRA, CHENNAI, KARNATAKA, GUJRAT, GOA, we are looking forward to spreading our wings of education across other cities as well. These efforts, on the other hand, will create the employment to 500 teaching and non-teaching staff, an opportunity for the enrolment of 10000 enthusiasts and create 100 women entrepreneurs by the year 2020.
